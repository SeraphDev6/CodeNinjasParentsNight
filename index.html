Why Teach Through Games?

  • Domain

    No matter what you are building you have to be able to understand the domain of the project. Games are a universal domain and allows us to spend more time focusing on what matters: learning to code

  • Engagement

    Interesting projects are going to drive more in depth learning, and an overall better experience

  • Creativity

    When students are encouraged and enabled to abstract away from the given material an create their own projects deeper learning and understanding is achieved.
